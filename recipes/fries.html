Fries

Description

fries are yummy, makes fries, but they are also pretty greasy and full of bad fat, so don't eat too much and too often of it !

Ingredients

  • Fries
  • Sunflower oil
  • Frying machine

Steps

  1. Warm the oil in the frying machine to 170 degrees Celsius
  2. Once oil is hot, machine will beep then blanch the fries in for about 3 to 4 minuts
  3. If you want you can now keep them like that for a while before you cook em right before serving
  4. Warm the oil at 180 degrees Celsius
  5. Once at temperature, put the fries in again, take them out when they float and are nice and shiny yellow
  6. Eat very quickly, cold fries are disgusting
