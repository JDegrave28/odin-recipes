Bacon and eggs

Description

Bacons and eggs are a delicious meal, that can be served at any time in the day.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted smoked bacon
  • Fresh farm eggs
  • Salt, pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Get the pan to hot, just not super hot, then put a tablespoon of olive oil in it. Wait for the oil to warm up
  2. Put as much bacon as u want, bacon is delicious, wait for it to start to become crispy
  3. Before the bacon is too crispy crack the eggs and put them in the pan, don't salt immediatly, but you can put the peppter in
  4. Once the white is ferm, and the yolks are nice and shiny, salt, and serve immediatly on a plate
